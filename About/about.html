About

AT QUADRO BARBEROS, WE BELIEVE THAT, AT ITS BEST, A BARBERSHOP IS A DEPENDABLE, TRUSTED NEIGHBORHOOD INSTITUTION.



Our primary goal is to build strong, long-term relationships with each of our clients.



Quadro Barberos of Manila is proud to be an independent, Manila-bred and operated store focused solely on serving the Manilenos.


Our unparalleled team of expert barbers has personal pride riding on each haircut and hot lather shave. No one on Barbers’ staff has been cutting hair fewer than 10 years. We’ve seen it all and can do it all. We are about substance and skill, not gimmicks. We like to have fun, but we take our work seriously.
