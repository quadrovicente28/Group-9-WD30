Haircuts in Manila

Quadros Barberso of Manila has provided award-winning men's haircuts and hot lather shaves to discerning Boston-area clients since 2006. We take tremendous pride in our work and perpetuating the fine art of classic barbering. Our strength and differentiation is manifest in the details: how intently we listen to what you want, how well the hair is tapered, how straight the neckline is, how even the sideburns are. You can't find a finer men's haircut in Boston. We would love the opportunity to be your Boston barber of choice.

The vintage photos and oak flooring spell tradition - and so do the master barbers with decades of experience. The superb cuts, hot-lather shaves and other services - combined with the welcoming vibe make this shop one of our favorites.
